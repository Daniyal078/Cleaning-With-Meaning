About Us

My name is Fernanda, founder of Cleaning With Meaning.

I truly believe that every place needs a touch of cleaning and love. I think that a clean home and a clean environment improves our health, and even our business. I’m passionate about helping people and making them happy with cleaning services. I know how important it is to have a clean home for everyone, and I also understand that people are very busy with their work and family. For this reason, I decided to establish my company in September 2019.

Cleaning With Meaning was created to help people, providing them with home wellness services and cleaning services for business owners.

Our business was founded on the following company values: integrity, commitment to our customers, passion, honesty, and accountability. We aspire to be the most valuable residential and commercial cleaning service.

We believe in the power of a clean environment to inspire productivity, health, and happiness. Aside from the benefits of having a clean house, the relationship between a clean house and mental health can reduce anxiety, bringing calmness and peace.
